# Peptide-MD

# 🧬 Optimize Your Wellness with AI-Powered Peptide Tracking

**Track, learn, and improve your health with personalized peptide insights.**

Peptide MD is an AI-powered wellness platform designed to simplify peptide tracking, learning, and performance optimization. By combining health data with intelligent insights, the app helps users make informed decisions and achieve measurable, sustainable wellness outcomes.

## ✨ Features

- 🧬 Personalized peptide tracking
- 🤖 AI-powered wellness insights
- 📊 Monitor health and performance metrics
- 😴 Track sleep scores and recovery
- 🩺 View biomarker reports and health data
- 🏃 Analyze activity and fitness metrics
- 📚 Educational resources on peptides and wellness
- 📱 Clean, modern, and user-friendly interface

## 📱 About the App

Peptide MD helps users navigate today's data-rich wellness landscape by bringing together peptide tracking, health metrics, and AI-powered recommendations in one intuitive platform.

Instead of managing multiple sources of health information, users can monitor their sleep, biomarker reports, activity levels, and wellness progress from a single dashboard. AI-generated insights transform complex data into practical recommendations, making performance optimization simple, measurable, and sustainable.

The app is ideal for:

- Health and wellness enthusiasts
- Fitness professionals
- Athletes
- Individuals tracking peptide protocols
- Anyone seeking personalized performance optimization

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Create your account.
2. Log your peptide protocol and wellness data.
3. Sync or enter health metrics such as sleep, activity, and biomarkers.
4. Receive AI-powered insights and personalized recommendations.
5. Track your progress and optimize your wellness journey over time.
